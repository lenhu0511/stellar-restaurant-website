Welcome To Stellar Restaurant

Stellar brings you fine dining without a fuss, in a casual yet sophisticated dining atmosphere in the heart of downtown Vietnam. We focus on making the best version of the classic European dishes. Every single element in the menu is carefully selected from the freshest in-season ingredients.

We are proud to receive TripAdvisor’s Certificate of Excellence 2019 & Travelers’ Choice Winner Award 2020. We are currently in the Top 10% of Restaurants Worldwide.
